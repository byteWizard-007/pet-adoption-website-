Why Adopt?

Adopting a pet changes two lives forever. Here are just a few reasons to open your home.

Save Animal Lives

By adopting, you're directly giving a second chance to a pet in need of a loving home.

Reduce Homeless Pets

You help free up space at shelters, allowing them to save more abandoned animals.

Improve Mental Health

Pets offer unconditional love, reducing stress, anxiety, and feelings of loneliness.

Gain Loyal Companionship

Adopted pets form incredibly strong bonds with their new families, offering lifelong loyalty.

Support Animal Welfare

Your adoption fee goes towards the shelter's mission to rescue and care for other animals.

Build a Loving Family

Pets bring joy, laughter, and a sense of completeness to families of all sizes.

Adoption Process

Bringing your new best friend home is easier than you think.

Step 1: Browse Available Pets

Look through our gallery of adorable pets and find the one that steals your heart.

Step 2: Submit Application

Fill out our simple adoption form to tell us a bit about your lifestyle and home.

Step 3: Schedule Meeting

Meet the pet in person or virtually to ensure a perfect match for both of you.

Step 4: Home Verification

A quick verification to ensure your home is a safe and suitable environment.

Step 5: Approval Process

Once everything checks out, we'll finalize the adoption paperwork and details.

Step 6: Take Your Pet Home

Welcome your new family member home and begin your beautiful journey together!

Adoption FAQ

Everything you need to know about the adoption process.

Start by browsing our available pets online. Once you find a match, fill out the adoption application. We will then schedule a meeting, conduct a brief home verification, and process the paperwork before you take your new friend home.

You will need a valid government-issued ID (Aadhar, PAN, or Passport), a recent utility bill as proof of address, and a signed copy of the adoption agreement.

Yes, all pets on our platform are fully vaccinated, dewormed, and have undergone a comprehensive health check by our certified veterinarians before being listed for adoption.

We currently prioritize local adoptions to ensure we can complete the necessary home verification and follow-ups. However, case-by-case exceptions are made for nearby cities.

Adoption fees vary depending on the pet's age, species, and medical requirements. Typically, it ranges from ₹2,000 to ₹5,000, which covers vaccinations, microchipping, and neutering/spaying.

The entire process usually takes between 3 to 7 business days from the moment you submit your application to the final approval and take-home day.
